Medical Expenses Analysis in Japan

Apr 2025
A visual explanation of rapid increase in social security expenditures, a critical social issue in Japan.

Trends in National Medical Expenditures (1954-2022)

Japan's medical expenses have increased rapidly alongside the aging society. As seen in the graph, there was particularly rapid growth from the 1970s to 1990s, followed by continued gradual increases. By 2022, expenses reached approximately 46.7 trillion yen, representing a critical challenge for social security.
